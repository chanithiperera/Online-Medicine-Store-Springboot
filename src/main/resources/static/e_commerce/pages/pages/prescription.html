Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about prescription orders

We accept prescriptions for all FDA-approved medications from licensed healthcare providers. This includes prescriptions for chronic conditions, acute illnesses, and specialty medications. We do not accept prescriptions for controlled substances online due to regulatory requirements.

Our pharmacy team typically verifies prescriptions within 1-2 hours during business hours (9am-9pm). Prescriptions submitted outside these hours will be processed the next business day. You'll receive an email or SMS once your prescription is verified.

Yes, we accept both traditional paper prescriptions (uploaded as images or scans) and electronic prescriptions (eRx) sent directly from your doctor's office. For e-prescriptions, you can provide your doctor with our pharmacy details to send the prescription directly to us.

For prescription refills, you can either upload a new prescription from your doctor or request us to contact your doctor for authorization if refills are remaining. Our system will automatically notify you when it's time to refill your medications.
